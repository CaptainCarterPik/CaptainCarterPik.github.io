  • about this site

    • In the Esums class of new media we modeled the mysterious black box to find out what is inside, what is the black box you may ask the mysterious black box is a well you know a black box, with a kind of contraption inside the amount of water that comes out varies sometimes the amount is more than what you put in sometimes its less and sometimes it stays the exact same so, we put that to the test by pouring water in the black box funnel and analysing the data.

    My Claim + Evidence

    My claim here is that inside the black box are two tubes that split into different directions one leads directly to the outside while the other leads to some kind of reservoir where it acts like a dam, it fills up until the water can properly reach the tube and it all drains out and thats what my team did using vinyl tubes, a funnel, tape and half of a plastic measuring cup we created a mock-up of the incredible black box and we did a couple of trial runs and luckily it worked!
